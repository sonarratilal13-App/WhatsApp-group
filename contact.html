Common Issues

Here are solutions to common problems:

My group submission was rejected

Group submissions may be rejected if they violate our content guidelines, contain inappropriate content, or if the WhatsApp link is invalid. Please review our Terms of Service and ensure your group complies with all guidelines before resubmitting.

I found an inappropriate group on your platform

We take content moderation seriously. Please use the contact form to report the specific group, including the group name and a description of the issue. Our team will review and take appropriate action.

How long does group approval take?

Group approval typically takes 24-48 hours. During peak times, it may take slightly longer. You'll receive an email notification once your group is approved or if any issues need to be addressed.

Can I update my group information after submission?

Currently, you'll need to resubmit your group with the updated information. We're working on a feature that will allow users to edit their approved groups directly.

Do you have an API for developers?

We're currently developing an API for developers. If you're interested in early access or partnership opportunities, please contact us with details about your project.
