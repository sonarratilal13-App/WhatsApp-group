Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2023

1. Information We Collect

GroupAdda collects minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Group Information: When you submit a WhatsApp group, we collect the group name, description, category, and invite link.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our website to improve user experience.
  • Technical Information: Like most websites, we collect standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To display WhatsApp groups on our platform
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate with users about their submissions
  • To ensure compliance with our terms of service

3. Information Sharing

GroupAdda does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With service providers who assist in operating our website (under confidentiality agreements)
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to WhatsApp groups and other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact page.

7. Cookies

GroupAdda uses cookies to enhance user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
